About OLX Group

Our Mission

At OLX Group, our mission is to empower people to upgrade their lives. We aim to make it simple for individuals and businesses to buy and sell goods and services, connecting them with their local communities.

Our History

OLX Group was founded in 2006 with the vision of creating an online marketplace that would allow people to easily buy and sell used items. Over the years, we have expanded our services to cater to various categories and countries, becoming one of the world's leading online classifieds platforms.

Our Values

  • Customer-Centricity: We prioritize the needs and satisfaction of our customers.
  • Innovation: We continuously strive to improve and innovate our platform and services.
  • Trust and Safety: We are committed to creating a safe and trustworthy environment for our users.
  • Empowerment: We believe in empowering individuals and businesses to succeed.
  • Community: We foster a sense of community and connection among our users.
