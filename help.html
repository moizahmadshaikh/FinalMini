Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I post an ad on OLX?

    To post an ad on OLX, follow these steps:

    1. Go to the OLX website or open the OLX app.
    2. Click on the "Post Free Ad" button.
    3. Select the appropriate category for your ad.
    4. Provide the necessary details and include clear photos.
    5. Review your ad and click "Submit" to post it.

  • How can I contact the seller?

    To contact a seller on OLX, use the following steps:

    1. Open the ad for the item you are interested in.
    2. Click on the "Contact Seller" button.
    3. Fill out the contact form with your details and message.
    4. Click "Send" to send your message to the seller.

  • What should I do if I encounter a suspicious ad or user?

    If you come across a suspicious ad or user on OLX, please take the following actions:

    1. Click on the "Report" button located on the ad page.
    2. Select the appropriate reason for your report.
    3. Provide any additional information that may be helpful.
    4. Click "Submit" to report the ad or user to OLX for review.
